Alyssa Groeneveld is a designer that grew up in Rotterdam and Amsterdam.
Before she started her MA menswear at Central Saint Martins (London) she studied Fashion design at Willem de Kooning in Rotterdam. During her masters she got to learn herself the draping technique with existing garments that she gets sponsored by several brands and/or that she buys via joblots on Ebay. Her collections are made out of zero patterns with influences of a mix of sportswear and formal wear influences. The concept of her work are based on groups of man that express their emotions in a particular environments.
